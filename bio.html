Matt Kirk is:

Analytical:

I love to deconstruct everything.

When I was 7, I took my parents' VCR apart. They got upset. They cooled off when I put it back together for them again and it worked.

When I went to the University of Washington I earned both a mathematics degree and quantitative economics degree.

My career started doing quantitative finance research. My research was on the effects of volatility on tax-loss harvesting as well as momentum and reversal in emergent economies. The initial research I did has turned into a Russell Index called CrossVol (crossvol.com).

Data production is growing at an alarming rate. My skill with analysis can help you become more adept with data.

Teams need focus. I can help a team overcome challenges by focusing on what matters, results.
