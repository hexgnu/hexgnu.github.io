Analytical:

I've always been analytical.

I started my career at Parametric Portfolio Associates as a quantitative research analyst. While there I studied the effects of volatility as it relates to tax-loss harvesting. The basic idea is how higher volatility will yield better harvesting opportunities for Parametric's clients. This initial research was turned into a Russell Index after I left called CrossVol (crossvol.com).

This theme has continued throughout my career as I have carved a niche for myself doing mathematics and statistics as it relates to programming. Recently I was the Chief Scientist of SocialVolt in Kansas City. Where I was in charge of running all research and development as it relates to social media data.

Today I help business tackle hard to analyze problems with my company Modulus 7. I am in the unique position of being one of the few individuals who knows both Ruby and math at an expert level.
